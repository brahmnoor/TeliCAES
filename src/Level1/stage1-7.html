Stage 1-7 Tips for correct usage of subject-verb agreement in academic context

  • 1. The number (i.e. singularity or plurality) of a noun affects its subject-verb agreement.
  • 2. You should identify the subject head noun first, if it is separated by a long phrase/clause from the main verb.
  • 3. Pay special attention to the common errors in subject-verb agreement outlined in this unit.
    • You may also go to Purdue Writing Lab: https://cgi.duke.edu/web/sciwriting/index.php?action=passive_voice for more examples.
