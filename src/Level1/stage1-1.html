Stage 1-1 What is Subject-Verb Agreement?

A subject is necessary for completing a sentence as it indicates the focus of the construction. It usually occupies the beginning position of the sentence. The subject must agree with the verb going after it i.e. a singular noun must agree with a singular verb, whereas a plural noun must agree with a plural verb. For example:

  • 1. That particular minority group in Hong Kong does not have enough financial support from the HKSAR government.
  • 2. Minority groups in Hong Kong do not have enough financial support from the HKSAR government.
  • In 1. we use the verb does as ‘ That particular minority group ’ is a singular subject
  • In 2. do instead of does should be selected as ‘ Minority groups in Hong Kong ’ is a plural subject.
