Stage 5-4 Mission 1

In each pair of sentences below, one is written in active voice and the other, in passive. Recall what you have learnt in Section 1 and decide if the information should be presented in active or passive voice. Check the corresponding checkbox to indicate your choice.

  • 1.a.
    We heated the mixture to 98°C for approximately 60 minutes and then allowed it to cool.
  • 1.b.
    The mixture was heated to 98°C for approximately 60 minutes and then allowed to cool.
  • 2.a.
    Most of the participants responded well to the new drug.
  • 2.b.
    The new drug was well-responded to by most participants.
  • 3.a.
    The saplings were kept in a humidified chamber overnight to prevent them from drying out.
  • 3.b.
    The researchers kept the saplings in a humidified chamber overnight to prevent them from drying out.
  • 4.a.
    Escherichia has been one of the most extensively used bacteria in genetic engineering.
  • 4.b.
    Scientists in the field of genetic engineering have used Escherichia as one of the most extensively used bacteria in their research.
  • 5.a.
    The World Health Organization issued a warning against the increasing sugar consumption globally.
  • 5.b.
    A warning against the increasing sugar consumption globally was issued by the World Health Organization.
  • 6.a.
    Adequate consumption of carbohydrates is needed to meet the demands of a high intensity physical training programme.
  • 6.b.
    Athletes or fitness enthusiasts need to consume adequate carbohydrates to meet the demands of a high intensity physical training programme.
  • 7.a.
    I consider such a sampling method inaccurate and outdated in general.
  • 7.b.
    Such a sampling method is considered inaccurate and outdated in general.
