Stage 7-7 Tips for Nominalizing Effectively

Tips for Nominalizing Effectively
  • 1. Nominalization is a formation process of nouns (or nominal phrases) in formal academic writing,
  • 2. Nouns (or nominal phrases) can be created by adding -ing or suffixes such as -ism, –ness and –ity or a definite article “the” to the root word (i.e. the base of a word – in this case, verbs or adjectives),
  • 3. Nominalization can be achieved by converting a verb or an adjective directly to a noun, and
  • 4. Nominalization should be used with caution; excessive use of nominalization may be counterproductive and thus the meanings of a sentence might be obfuscated.
