Stage 7-2 Functions of Nominalization

  • 1. In the process of nominalization, you can make your writing more concise and formal by turning verbs (actions, events or states) to nouns (concepts, qualities or activities).
  • 2. Nominalized sentences can help you avoid using the same vocabulary and wording repeatedly which gives you more variety in your academic writing.
  • 3. Nominalization is a very important linguistic feature in academic writing. It not only strengthens the formality (or seriousness) and objectivity of the writing style (or tone) itself, but also helps to construct a professional identity of the writer and to express complex ideas, perception and knowledge.
