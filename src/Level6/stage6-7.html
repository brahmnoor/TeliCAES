Stage 6-7 Tips for Effective Hedging and Boosting

  • 1. Evaluate the degree of proposition in your writing; what is the certainty level you want to express?
  • 2. Adjust your certainty levels by selecting the suitable hedges / boosters for that sentence.
  • 3. Do not hedge every sentence -- Over-hedging will weaken your argument!
  • 4. Do not overuse boosters -- You may become a target for criticism!
  • 5. A thesis statement that hedges too much suggests to your readers that you are unsure of your stance!
  • 6. If you have found sufficient evidence and formulated sufficient reasons, you can state your claim with confidence with suitable boosters, without using hedges!
