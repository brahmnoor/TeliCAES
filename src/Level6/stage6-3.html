Stage 6-3 Functions of Boosters

According to Hyland (1998), boosters serve the following purposes in academic writing:

  • 1. Increase the force of claims
  • 2. Emphasize the magnitude of quality or quantity
  • 3. Help writers to assert their arguments with confidence

  • Compare the examples below:

  • Numerouseducators claim that the low recognition of the associate's degree was obviously due to the perceived insufficient quality of these programs.

  • If you have found sufficient evidence and formulated sufficient reasons, you can state your claim with confidence with suitable boosters, without using hedges. However, always consider the strength of your evidence when choosing whether to hedge or boost.
