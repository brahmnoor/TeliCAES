Stage 2-6 Tips for Effective Hedging and Boosting

  • 1. Keep your audience in mind when you are writing; can your ideas be followed by someone else?
  • 2. Be aware of the function of the linking word you have selected. Is the meaning carried across effectively in your writing?
  • 3. Do not quantify linking words. ‘The more the better’ does NOT apply here! Again, pay attention to the meaning you are trying to create.
