Stage 3-7 Tips on Using Pronoun Referencing Effectively

    Every pronoun should refer clearly to a specific antecedent that has been mentioned and is close by. The three major features of pronoun referencing covered in this unit are:

  • 1. “Agreement” between the pronoun and the information to which it refers.
  • 2. Using “it”, “this” and “which” to refer to previously mentioned ideas/information.
  • 3. Using “it” as an “empty” reference or a forward-looking reference to something to be discussed later in the sentence.
