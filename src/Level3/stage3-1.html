Stage 3-1 Three Usages of Pronouns in Academic Writing

  • Pronouns refer to information or ideas that appear earlier or later in the text. There are THREE major areas regarding the usage of pronouns in academic writing:
  • 1. Reference agreement.
  • 2. Using “it,” “this” and “which” to refer to previously mentioned information.
  • 3. Using “it” as an “empty” reference, or a forward-looking reference to something to be discussed later in the sentence.
